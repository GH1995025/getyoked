Monster Survivors

Fight endless waves of monsters and survive as long as possible in this action-packed adventure!

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you'll face endless waves of monsters. Your goal is simple but challenging: survive as long as possible while defeating hordes of enemies.

As you progress through the game, you'll unlock new abilities, weapons, and power-ups that will help you combat increasingly difficult monsters. The game features procedurally generated levels, ensuring that no two playthroughs are exactly the same.

With its engaging gameplay mechanics and progressively challenging difficulty, Monster Survivors offers hours of entertainment for players of all skill levels.

How to Play

Controls are simple and intuitive:

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Your character automatically attacks nearby enemies
  • Collect experience orbs to level up and choose new abilities
  • Pick up power-ups to enhance your character's abilities

The longer you survive, the stronger you become - but beware, the monsters also get tougher over time!
